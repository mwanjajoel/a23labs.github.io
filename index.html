About Us

We leverage technology to address a diverse set of real world challenges by creating time critical solutions that solve business and social needs on mobile, hardware and cloud platforms.

Our process involves creating simple to use innovative tools that enrich the lives of people.

We are constantly inspired by the stories of people who have used the tools we created to better their lives.

We believe in creating the right tools for people to use to change their lives, and those around them.
